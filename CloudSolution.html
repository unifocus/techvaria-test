Business & Data Intelligence

In a nutshell, Business Intelligence (also simply addressed as BI) refers to a set of applications and processes that help analyses a company’s data, activities, projects, scope and revenue. Several aspects fall under the category of Business Intelligence. These are:

Strategy and planning : These days’ businesses are by and large driven by data. After all, data helps companies take informed decisions that have a definitive source and cause. Techvaria provides complete strategizing and planning services to help you understand techniques on how you can better utilize your data. This can assist you in monetizing your data, understand issues within your network, acquire better resources and plan your corporate finance better.

Performance Management : Techvaria possesses the expertise to help you maximize your data output to supervise business performance. This can include the company itself, its divisions, projects, or its personnel. Our Performance Management consulting can help automate the discovery of such aspects.

Big Data : Big Data is easily understood as those data types that are large in quantity, rate at which it changes, or its type. To handle big data, you need an experienced eye that can not only metaphorically ‘digest’ big data, but also present you with accurate awareness, a decisive approach and process automation. Techvaria provides the answer.

Data Visualization : For data to have a semblance of usable output and success, it needs to be presentable and understood. The visual medium is the most intuitive and meaningful in this regard. Techvaria provides key data visualization solutions that enables you to access your data and analytics through customizable dashboards, filters, parameters, graphs and more.


1. Analytical Solutions : (systems, applications, products) has made business processes simpler thanks to big data and analytics. Techvaria provides key business solutions in to address all such issues and help your projects witness successful deployment and enable business growth in the process.

2. Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) : Revenue, profit and financial management are some of the key aspects of a CFO’s (Chief Financial Officer) core result areas. Also, known as corporate performance management, EPM solutions give them better control over planning and strategy, which enables them to mobilise reports and enhance profit. EPM helps you obtain a better reporting structure, flexible and agile organisational structure, quicker planning, financial consolidation, and budgeting and forecasting. Higher revenue and better financial planning and management are additional benefits of this solution.

3. Business Intelligence : One of the most vital giveaways that business intelligence (or BI for the practiced hand) has is that it gives those who apply it, the power to visualise. Visualization also leads to be able to weave a narrative across a project. Interactive dashboards give one the opportunity to access data assuming multiple roles and obtain reports and analytics accordingly.

4. Business Objects Predictive Data Analytics : As the name suggests, this analysis helps business take an informed decision about their projects and processes. 7. Digital Boardroom : Digital Boardroom is a solution that is built to involve HANA and its cloud instance. Our solution enables clients using this solution to take data and report driven decisions.
