Project Management

a. PMP Trainings : we offer 35 hours of standard PMP training and other required support for candidates to obtain PMP certificates. Our instructors have proven record of 98% success rate.

b. Project Managers to Project Leaders : Custom-designed for Project Managers, this 2 - day (16 hour) course covers an array of aspects such as understanding client strategy, delivering value beyond the triple constraints of schedule, cost and scope, Stakeholdersâ€™ Management, Requirement Tractability and verification, business benefits measurements, conditions of satisfaction, managing organizational change, Enterprise transformation project management and more.

Another key aspect that the course touches upon is key skills required to be an able project leader. The environment for project management is complex and needs to have someone with reasoning skills, foresight, conviction, know-how and drive. For corporations, this course will deliver a leader who can ensure timely delivery of business value, with budgetary and auxiliary goals successfully attained. Individuals garner confidence and skills, with a definitive growth curve along their career path.

c. Project Financial Management : Tailored to suit the requisites of Project Managers and Senior Managers, in this 2 day (16-hour course), you will learn about the vital composition of financial management and planning. The course will touch upon instilling basic financial acumen, understanding project finance, the importance of risk contingencies, project costs case and forecast. This course emphasizes on the crucial pillars of financial factors, and the responsibilities of project leadership. Participants will learn how to view their projects and teams as financial units within an organization. They will also get a look at diverse terminologies and concepts that they can apply to their projects. Businesses that invest in acquiring this training for their personnel stand to gain a leader who will play a key role in achieving the financial goals of their projects.

d. Project Risk Management : This is another course targeted at Project Managers and Senior Managers. During this 2-day (16 hour) course, you will be apprised with the key factors of understanding risks, risk management cycle, risk identification, risk assessment and prioritization, risk management planning, risk response planning, contingency vs Risk reserve and more. Assessing potential risks and devising the methods of dealing with the same is necessary to ensure projects retain their profitability for an organization. This course helps project managers be proactive and optimally implement qualitative and quantitative methods, strategy and concepts to determine the impact of risks and how to mitigate their effect. Participants will get to learn about applying scalable concepts to project management, be more analytical, integrate technique and response, and develop their own plan among other things.

e. Stakeholder's Management Training : This course can be covered in 1 day (8 hours), but also has a more expansive, detailer version that extends to another day. As part of the curriculum in this course, you learn the skill of identifying who exactly comprise the stakeholders of a project or process. It gives you insights into what each stakeholder's expectations and requisites are, how to manage and interact with them, and how to collectively work to successfully complete the project.

f. Project Management Maturity Assessment : In this 2-day (16 hour) program, project managers and leaders get an opportunity to understand diverse levels and factors that determine the successful maturity of a project. One of the important take-aways for the participants is understanding the method of project assessment and the best practices for improving.

g. Delivering Value Through Project Management : This program lasts for the duration of just a day (8 hours) and is ideal for those who have just participated in Techvaria's Project Managers to Project Leaders program. It also helps you analyses the triple constraint (scope, schedule and cost) factor against the fourth constraint (quality).

Leadership Trainings

a. Building Effective Teams : A captain is as good as the team that hits the ground goes the old saying. This itself emphasizes the importance of having a good team that believes in timely delivery and going the extra mile to ensure successful deployment. The program that can be taken either across 1 day (8 hours) or 2 days (16 hours) helps you decipher how you can acquire the best resources the market must offer.

b. Managing Cross-cultural, global and diverse teams : It goes without saying that we live in a multicultural environment. Most companies in India have people hailing from diverse parts of the country. That apart this is also common in a number of global companies as well. This 1 day (8 hours) program helps project managers and senior managers understand how to handle diversity, utilize that to their advantage, team formation, flexible working style implementation, and situational management.

c. Managing Change in Organizations : This program too can be taken in two phases - 1 day (8 hours) or 2 days (16 hours) - or all at once. Change is deemed the biggest constant, and this program enables managers to understand how to affect change and how to deal with it as well. They get an understanding of how to adapt and work for greater delivery in future projects.

d. Decision Approach and Resolution : This is a day long (8 hours) course that is ideal for Senior Managers and Project Managers. It helps you understand the very basics of technique. These include the importance of setting goals, developing alternative plans, evaluating inputs and reports, evaluate issues, obtain approvals and decision arrival.

e. Conflict Management : Conflicts are a common occurrence in organizational behavior. And just as common as conflicts are, resolution is just as important in an organization. This is a 1 day (8 hour) course that throws light on what the most usual reasons for conflict are, causes for functional and dysfunctional conflicts, how to approach and address a conflict, and being proactive towards addressing conflicts.

f. Negotiating for Success : This program can be taken either across 1 day (8 hours) or 2 days (16 hours), and is perfectly designed for Senior Managers, Project Managers and Leaders of an organization. This program touches upon the key aspects of negotiation, its cycle, how to prepare for it, BATNA (Best alternative to a negotiated agreement) methods, the importance of holding influence, communicating with and managing powerful counterparts, and the importance of taking control in a negotiation process.

g. Presentation Skills for Leaders : The ability to present an idea, a report, or a formal update is a vital part of effective communication in the corporate sphere. Often, a great idea may never be greenlighted if it is not put across effectively. In this day, long (8 hour) course, you will be introduced to the importance of having a narrative in your presentation. It will help you imbibe logical reasoning in yourself and how you must apply it to your presentation skills. Additionally, the program will emphasize on the importance of eye contact, posture and movement, gestures, dressing, voice, humor, audience involvement, control and most importantly, preparation for a presentation.

h. Client Facing Skills : Tailored to suit the requisites of Delivery Managers primarily, this is a 2 -day (16 hours) program that apprises the participants on the importance of possessing the ability to confidently face and interact with a client. It touches upon understanding client viewpoints and requirements, the ability to listen and communicate, being able to communicate even remotely, being able to effectively present things, the importance of speaking out and knowing when to say no, understanding diversity in culture and more.
