Here are some key reasons for you to join Techvaria :

1. High growth curve : We understand and have imbibed the best giveaways of the startup culture and work environment. With this as an intrinsic part of our process, you possess a great opportunity to make steadfast growth. You will also get the opportunity to be a part of several pilots. You can emphatically claim that you were involved since the initial phase of the company and have contributed to its success. We're confident we'll achieve and so will you.

2. Exceptional learning curve : Techvaria's management possesses a cumulative experience of over 5 decades in technology, business solutions and services. Working with us, you will have the excellent opportunity to work with and learn from these established professionals. It will be a masterclass for all practical purposes. You will get to learn the intricacies of industry, management, domain and entrepreneurship.

3. Flexibility and creativity : We believe that champion performers need not be shackled. We allow the provision of flexible work hours to our employees. We also believe in fanning the fire of innovation and welcome our employees to innovate, create and provide key business solutions for their personal growth and the benefit of our clientele.

It has been our desire to solve problems and that has driven us to where we are. We think of ourselves as a company with no individuals, but with a team of people who like to find solutions to what ails most businesses across industries. If you conform to this, come aboard.

Customer/Technical Support

Requirements
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in the web hosting industry
  • Understanding of shared, reseller, vps and dedicated server hosting
  • Average experience with WHMCS, Kayako and OpenVZ
  • Understanding of open source software such as WordPress, Drupal, Joomla, etc.
  • Ability to quickly and efficiently handle web hosting issues such as DNS, FTP, PHP-based errors, etc.
  • Access to reliable high speed internet connection
  • Comfortable with speaking with the customers on the phone
